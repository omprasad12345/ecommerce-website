About Us

Welcome to Crafty Marketplace, your number one source for all things handmade and unique. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of crafts, with a focus on quality, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2024 by Omprasad jena, Crafty Marketplace has come a long way from its beginnings in a home office. When Jane first started out, her passion for helping crafters sell their handmade creations drove her to do intense research, quit her day job, and gave her the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a booming online store.

We now serve customers all over the world and are thrilled to be a part of the quirky, eco-friendly, fair trade wing of the e-commerce industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
